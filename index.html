Crystal Collector!

This is a game where you are given a random number to reach by hunting for crystals.

You are given a random number at the start of the game.

Notice that there are four crystals shown below -- you play the game by clicking on one of the crystals. Each time you click the same crystal you add a specific number of points to your score.

If you can make your score exactly equal to the the random number, you win! But you lose if your score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is unknown to you until you click on it. And each time you play a new game the crystals get new values.
